Family Law

Family law encompasses a wide range of legal issues that affect families. From marriage and divorce to child custody and adoption, understanding your rights and obligations is essential.

Key Areas of Family Law

  • Divorce and Separation
  • Child Custody and Visitation
  • Child Support
  • Adoption
  • Spousal Support (Alimony)
  • Domestic Violence Protection

The Divorce Process

  1. Filing for divorce
  2. Serving divorce papers
  3. Negotiating settlements
  4. Mediation (if necessary)
  5. Court proceedings (if unable to settle)
  6. Final divorce decree
