Criminal Defense

Criminal defense law protects the rights of individuals accused of committing crimes. Understanding your rights and the legal process is crucial if you or a loved one is facing criminal charges.

Types of Criminal Charges

  • Misdemeanors
  • Felonies
  • White-collar crimes
  • Drug offenses
  • Violent crimes
  • DUI/DWI

Your Rights When Arrested

  • Right to remain silent
  • Right to an attorney
  • Right to a fair trial
  • Protection against unreasonable searches and seizures
  • Right to know the charges against you
